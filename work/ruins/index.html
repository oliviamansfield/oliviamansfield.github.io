Ruins
2014 — Oil on Canvas

When you look, you really look, what do you see? Secrets of the deep and ghosts who those who have walked before us. The image of the horses head is one of great power, the three horsemen of the apocalypse, vengeance and sorrow. Fragility of life drowning, dying, yet the fluid water feeds a life new and pure.
