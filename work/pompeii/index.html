Pompeii
2014 — Oil on Canvas

Deep seated fascination with classical history specifically Greek and Roman conjures imagery of lost cities and civilisations. The eruption of Vesuvius in 79AD depicted with an almost surreal vision of the cites left behind in her wake. Apocalyptic nature, beauty in destruction, glory in death.
